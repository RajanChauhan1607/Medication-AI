# Medication-AI — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 24 June 2026**

Medication-AI ("Medication-AI", "we", "us", or "our") provides a mobile application that scans
handwritten prescriptions, organises your medicines, and reminds you to take them. This
Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you
have. By using Medication-AI, you agree to this Policy.

If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

---

## 1. Who we are

Medication-AI is operated by the Medication-AI team. For any privacy question or request, contact us at
**adityavalaki1110@gmail.com**.

---

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work.

**a) Account information**
- If you sign in with **Google**, we receive your **name** and **email address** from Google.
- If you continue as a **guest**, we create an anonymous account tied to your device; we do
  not collect your name or email.

**b) Health and medication information you provide**
- Photographs of prescriptions you scan or upload.
- Medicine names, strengths, forms, dosage schedules, durations, and instructions.
- Your medication adherence (which doses you mark as taken, snoozed, or skipped).
- Profile details you enter: age, gender, and meal times.

**c) Emergency (SOS) contacts**
- Names, relationships, and phone numbers of contacts you choose to add.

**d) Technical and usage information**
- Limited scan-usage logs (a per-user identifier and timestamps) used to enforce fair-use
  limits and keep the service running. We do **not** use advertising identifiers and we do
  **not** track you across other apps or websites.

We do **not** collect your location, contacts list, microphone audio, or browsing history.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information to:
- Read your prescription and turn it into a structured medicine list (see Section 4).
- Save your medicines, schedule, and reminders, and sync them to your account.
- Send you on-device reminders/alarms to take your medicines.
- Let you call your emergency contacts.
- Protect the service from abuse (rate limiting) and fix problems.

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your health data for
advertising.

---

## 4. AI processing of prescriptions

To read a prescription, the photo is sent securely to our server, which forwards it to
**Google's Gemini API** for text extraction. Google processes the image to return the
medicine details and acts as our processor for this step. We do not send your name, email,
or contacts to the AI — only the prescription image. The extracted result is shown to you
for review and correction before anything is saved.

> AI extraction can be inaccurate. Always check the medicines and doses before saving, and
> confirm with your doctor or pharmacist.

---

## 5. How your information is stored and secured

- Your data is stored using **Supabase** (our database, authentication, and file storage
  provider) on secured cloud infrastructure.
- Data is encrypted **in transit** (HTTPS/TLS) and **at rest**.
- Every record is protected by **row-level security**, so your data is accessible only to
  your own authenticated account.
- Prescription images are kept in a **private storage area** scoped to your account.
- Our AI key and server credentials are held **only on the server**, never inside the app.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to
protect your information.

---

## 6. Who we share information with

We share information only with the service providers that make Medication-AI work:
- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and file storage.
- **Google** — Google Sign-In (if you choose it) and the Gemini API (prescription reading).

We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and
security of our users and the service. We do not share your information with advertisers.

## 7. Your choices and rights

- **Access & correction:** You can view and edit your medicines, profile, and contacts in
  the app at any time.
- **Deletion:** You can delete medicines and contacts in the app. To delete your entire
  account and associated data, email **adityavalaki1110@gmail.com** and we will remove it.
- **Guest data:** Guest (anonymous) data is tied to your device session; signing out or
  uninstalling may make it unrecoverable.
- **Notifications:** You can turn reminders on or off per medicine, and control system
  notification permissions in your device settings.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, portability,
or objection). Contact us to exercise them.

## 8. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the
service. When you delete your account, we delete your personal data, except where we must
retain limited records to comply with legal obligations.
